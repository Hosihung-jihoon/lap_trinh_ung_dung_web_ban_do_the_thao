Training for your first 5K can be daunting, but with the right mindset and preparation, anyone can cross that finish line. This guide covers everything from gear selection to weekly training schedules designed for beginners.

Getting Started: The Essentials

Before you hit the pavement, you need to ensure you have the right equipment. Running is a high-impact sport, and protecting your joints starts with your feet.

The 8-Week Training Schedule

Consistency is key. Our 8-week plan gradually builds your endurance using a mix of running and walking intervals.

  • check_circle Week 1-2: Run 1 min, Walk 2 mins (Repeat 20 mins)
  • check_circle Week 3-4: Run 3 mins, Walk 2 mins (Repeat 25 mins)
  • check_circle Week 5-6: Run 8 mins, Walk 2 mins (Repeat 30 mins)

Recovery & Nutrition

Rest days are when your muscles repair and strengthen. Pair rest with proper hydration and protein intake.
