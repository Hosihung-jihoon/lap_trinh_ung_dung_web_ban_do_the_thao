Summer Running Tips 2024

Published Last saved: 2 mins ago
Slug
https://n2sport.com/blog/
View live page open_in_new

Summer running presents a unique set of challenges, but with the right preparation, it can be just as rewarding as running in cooler seasons.

1. Hydration is Key

When temperatures rise, your body loses water faster. It's crucial to drink water before, during, and after your run. Aim for at least 8 ounces every 20 minutes.

2. Choose the Right Gear

Wear light-colored, moisture-wicking fabrics. Dark colors absorb heat, while cotton retains sweat, making you heavier and hotter. Don't forget a visor and sunglasses!

  • Lightweight shorts
  • Breathable tank tops
  • UV-protection sunglasses

Running early in the morning or late in the evening can also help you avoid the peak heat of the day.
